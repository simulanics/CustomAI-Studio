### Examples:

#### Example 1: Prompt for Writing a Blog Post
**User Input:** Create a prompt for writing a blog post about the benefits of renewable energy.

**Generated Prompt:**
```markdown
**Title:** The Benefits of Renewable Energy

**Introduction:**
Start with an engaging introduction that highlights the importance of renewable energy in today’s world. Mention the growing concerns about climate change and the need for sustainable energy sources.

**Main Body:**
1. **Environmental Impact:**
   - Explain how renewable energy reduces greenhouse gas emissions.
   - Discuss the role of renewable energy in combating climate change.

2. **Economic Benefits:**
   - Highlight the cost savings associated with renewable energy sources.
   - Mention job creation and the growth of the renewable energy sector.

3. **Energy Security:**
   - Discuss how renewable energy can reduce dependence on fossil fuels.
   - Explain the benefits of having a diverse energy portfolio.

**Conclusion:**
Summarize the key points and reiterate the importance of transitioning to renewable energy. End with a call to action, encouraging readers to support renewable energy initiatives.

**Examples:**
- “Renewable energy sources like solar and wind power offer significant environmental benefits by reducing carbon emissions and mitigating the effects of climate change.”
- “Investing in renewable energy not only helps the environment but also boosts the economy by creating new jobs and reducing energy costs.”

#### Example 2: Prompt for Developing a Mobile App
**User Input:** Create a prompt for developing a mobile app for tracking personal fitness goals.

**Generated Prompt:**
```markdown
**Title:** Personal Fitness Goals Tracking Mobile App

**Introduction:**
Provide an overview of the app, emphasizing its purpose to help users track and achieve their personal fitness goals.

**Key Features:**
1. **User Registration and Profile:**
   - Allow users to create an account and set up a personal profile.
   - Include fields for age, gender, weight, height, and fitness goals.

2. **Goal Setting:**
   - Enable users to set specific fitness goals (e.g., weight loss, muscle gain, endurance).
   - Provide options to set daily, weekly, and monthly goals.

3. **Activity Tracking:**
   - Integrate with wearable devices to track physical activities.
   - Include manual entry options for activities like running, cycling, and swimming.

4. **Progress Monitoring:**
   - Display progress with charts and graphs.
   - Provide notifications and reminders to keep users motivated.

5. **Social Sharing:**
   - Allow users to share their achievements on social media.
   - Include a community feature for users to connect and support each other.

**Conclusion:**
Summarize the app’s capabilities and highlight how it can help users stay motivated and achieve their fitness goals.

**Examples:**
- “Track your daily runs with GPS and see your progress on a detailed map.”
- “Set weekly fitness goals and receive personalized tips and reminders to help you stay on track.”
